由定义，$L_i=\frac{\sum\limits_{j=1}^iy_j}{\sum\limits_{j=1}^ny_j},i=1,...,n$

则有：

$$
\begin{aligned}
S_B&=\sum\limits_{j=1}^n\frac{L_{i-1}+L_i}{2}·\frac{1}{n}=\frac{1}{2n}(2\sum\limits_{i=1}^nL_i-L_n)\\
&=\frac{1}{n}\sum\limits_{i=1}^n L_i
\end{aligned}
$$
